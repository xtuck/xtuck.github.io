LAN Share Pro Privacy Policy

This policy explains how LAN Share Pro handles information.

Effective date: 2026-03-09

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to LAN Share Pro (the "App"), including the macOS desktop app and its LAN web access page.

2. Information We Process

To provide LAN file sharing, the App may process the following data locally on your device:

  • Files you choose to import, upload, download, preview, or delete, including file names and folder paths.
  • LAN connection data (such as client IP, host IP, and port) for access, isolation, and status display.
  • Authentication data, such as temporary passcodes and session cookies.
  • Operation logs (for example: service start/stop, login, upload, download, delete, HTTPS events).
  • App settings (such as port, concurrency limits, cache limit, language, and security options).

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide upload, download, preview, and delete features on your local network.
  • To enforce authentication, path safety, directory isolation, and storage/concurrency controls.
  • To show service status and access methods (QR code and local URL).
  • To support troubleshooting through local logs.

4. Storage and Retention

  • The App does not provide an official cloud relay service; data is stored on your local host device.
  • Shared files are stored in a local app cache directory managed by the App.
  • Settings are stored locally on your device.
  • Logs are stored locally and may be rotated when reaching configured limits.
  • You can delete files/logs in the App or uninstall the App to remove local data.

5. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not upload your file content to our cloud servers.
  • Transfers occur between your devices on your LAN unless you configure external exposure yourself.

6. Permissions and Network Access

  • File access is used to read files you choose and write them into shared folders.
  • Local network access is used to host LAN upload/download services.
  • Clients can access via QR code or by entering local IP:port URL in a browser.
  • If you expose the service to the public internet (for example, port forwarding or reverse proxy), you are responsible for the related security risks.

7. Security

  • Supports temporary passcode and session-based access control.
  • Applies path sanitization and boundary checks to reduce unauthorized path access risks.
  • Supports optional HTTPS with self-signed certificates.
  • Please use trusted networks and manage device access responsibly.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect children's personal information.

9. Your Controls

  • You can stop the service at any time and change security/network settings.
  • You can delete shared files and local logs.
  • You can uninstall the App to stop future data processing.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this policy to reflect feature changes or legal requirements. The latest version appears on this page.
